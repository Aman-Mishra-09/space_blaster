❓ How to Play

🎯 Objective: Destroy enemies and survive as long as possible!


Controls:

⬅️ ➡️ Arrow Keys / A, D — Move ship

🔫 Space / W / ↑ — Shoot

⏸️ P / Escape — Pause


Power-ups:

🛡️ Shield — Temporary invincibility

⚡ Rapid Fire — Faster shooting

🔱 Multi-Shot — Triple bullets

💚 Health — Restore health

💥 Nuke — Destroy all enemies


Enemies:

👾 Basic — Simple movement

💀 Fast — Quick and agile

🛸 Tank — High health, worth more points

👹 Boss — Appears every 5 levels!
